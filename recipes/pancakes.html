Pancakes Recipe

Pancakes are a delicious and popular breakfast dish enjoyed by people all over the world. They are a type of flat, round, and typically thin cake made from a simple batter that consists of basic ingredients like flour, eggs, milk, and a leavening agent such as baking powder. This mixture is whisked together to create a smooth and slightly thick consistency.

Ingredients

  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Butter or Oil
  • Vanilla Extract (optional)

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk together milk, egg, melted butter or oil.
  3. Pour the wet mixture into the dry mixture and stir until just combined. Don't overmix; it's okay if there are lumps.
  4. Preheat a greased pan over medium-high heat.
  5. Pour batter onto the pan to form pancakes.
  6. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown on both sides.
  7. Serve with your favorite toppings.
